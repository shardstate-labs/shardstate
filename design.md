# DESIGN SYSTEM — SHARDSTATE

## Tone
- Dark
- Oppressive
- Urban decay
- System-controlled world
- Divided zones

## Core Theme
- Humanity exploited through PULSOS
- PROTOCOL clan (Goverment) as invisible control
- Clans represent different survival strategies

---

## Visual Identity

### Base
- Dark matte surfaces
- Industrial + organic fusion
- Minimal glow

### Clans Identity and Colors

Clan-specific identity and colors:
NEXUS — Color: #6B5CE7
Dark cyberpunk street augmentations, illegal implants visible under skin glowing purple/blue, industrial scrap armor, neon-lit alley atmosphere, body modification aesthetic
TIDECALL — Color: #00B4D8
Ocean nomads, bioluminescent tattoos glowing teal/blue, weathered waterproof clothing, seashell and coral adornments, tribal maritime aesthetic, salt-weathered skin
ASHBORN — Color: #FF6B35
Pyromaniac religious cult, ritual burn scars, ember-red robes with flame motifs, ash-covered skin, glowing eyes like burning coal, ceremonial torch weapons
ERRVOID — Color: #B5179E
Nihilist hackers, dark hooded figures, glitch aesthetic, fragmented digital patterns on skin, purple/black palette, keyboards and screens integrated into body, void portals visible behind them
VAULT — Color: #F7B731
Post-apocalyptic aristocracy, impeccable tailoring over wasteland scarcity, gold jewelry and accessories, haughty expressions, bunker-chic fashion, surrounded by rare pre-fracture objects
MYCELIUM — Color: #2ECC71
Human-fungal fusion, mushroom caps growing from shoulders, green/brown bioluminescent spores floating, bark-like skin texture, nature reclaiming the human form, forest floor atmosphere
IRONPACT — Color: #E74C3C
Tribal mercenaries, mixed armor from salvaged parts, war paint on face, tribal tattoos, powerful physique, weapons made from ruins, simplistic but intimidating warrior aesthetic
SYNTHOS — Color: #3498DB
Techno-utopian scientists, clean lab coats over advanced tech suits, drones and floating screens around them, blue holographic readouts, calm analytical expressions, hope in their eyes amid ruins
LOOPKIN — Color: #9B59B6
Time-displaced warriors, clocks and hourglasses as armor elements, afterimages visible around them, purple/silver temporal distortion aura, anachronistic mix of historical and futuristic clothing
PHANTOM — Color: #1ABC9C
Shadow operatives, dark tactical suits with no visible seams, partial cloaking active, teal energy lines as their only color, hidden faces or masks, surveillance drones, minimal but precise aesthetic
FREQUENZ — Color: #F39C12
Post-apocalyptic artists, vibrant spray paint on face and arms, improvised stage costumes, LED lights woven into clothing, expressive chaotic poses, musical instruments as weapons, colorful chaos
PROTOCOL — Color: #7F8C8D
Military government remnants, pressed uniforms despite apocalypse, insignias and rank symbols, controlled expressions, standard-issue weapons maintained immaculately, authority in their posture
ECHO — Color: #F5BC94
Anomalous, alien, gods, non-human, disconnected from system, visually "wrong", less structured than other clans
-Each clan must feel visually distinct and unique
-ECHO must feel visually disconnected from system and visually "wrong"

## Cards

-base cards: 12 cards per clan (excluding ECHO)
-special cards: 10 cards for ECHO

Structure priority:
1. Stats (clear, readable)
2. Clan identity (visual layer)
3. Ability text (secondary)
4. Clan bonus text (secondary)

Rules:
- No generic gradients
- Avoid symmetry
- Each clan must feel unique

---

## Combat UI

- Instant feedback
- Clear damage resolution
- No ambiguity

---

## UX Principles

- Every action must feel impactful
- No hidden mechanics
- No unnecessary steps

---

## Anti-Patterns

- Generic sci-fi UI
- Overused neon gradients
- Clean corporate dashboards
- Repetitive layouts

---

## Goal

UI should feel like:

"A controlled system extracting value from players, not designed for comfort but for function"
