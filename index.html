Combat System

How Battles Work

01

Build Your Deck

Choose 8 cards across up to 2 clans. Each battle draws 4 cards from your deck — plan for variance.

02

Play Pulsos — Wager Your Multiplier

Each round you assign Pulsos to a card. More Pulsos = higher ATQ (ATQ = Power × (1 + Pulsos)). First to drain the opponent's 12 HP wins.

03

Clan Bonuses Activate

Running 3+ cards of the same clan unlocks a passive bonus. NEXUS crushes enemy ATQ. VAULT ignores Pulsos. ECHO rewrites rules entirely.

04

Abilities Change Everything

Each card has a unique ability — Poison, Weaken, Copy, Stop. Reading the battlefield is half the game.
